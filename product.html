TATA Nexon EV 18 Lakhs India's second-most affordable EV is offered by Tata Motors in the form of the Nexon EV. The compact SUV features a 30.2 kWh Li-ion battery with a claimed range of 312 km on a full charge. Power output for the Nexon EV is rated 129 ps while torque is at a healthy 245 Nm. The battery can be charged from zero to 80 % in just one hour using a DC fast charger while the regular 15 A socket will do the same job in about eight hours

Tata Tigor EV 12 Lakhs It is the most affordable passenger EV in India. The car bears a similar design to the Tigor sedan with some subtle changes and the key change is the electric powertrain replacing the internal combustion engine. The company claims a range of 306 km on a full charge and the car offers both normal and fast-charging options. The car is equipped with a 26kWh battery pack and offers a peak power of 75 hp and 170 Nm of torque.
